This python script is used to automatically add comments on a questions on HackerEarth platform. 
We need to provide the following details:-
  i) Username of HackerEarth account in line no. 33.
  ii) Password of the same account in line no. 34.
  iii) List of link of questions in line no. 35.
  iv) List of corresponding titles in line no. 36.
  v) List of corresponding comments in line no. 37.
  vi) Address of the chrome drivers store in your pc in line no. 38.

After providing these details you just need to run the script and comments will be automatically added on an average speed of 4 seconds per comment.

To download the chrome driver visit https://chromedriver.chromium.org/downloads and download the drivers based on your chrome version and pc specification.
